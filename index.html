My name is Pedro Alejandro Cabra-Acela, I hold a B.A and a M.A in economics at Universidad de los Andes, Colombia. For the past years, I have worked on research and consulting projects, as well as a TA. I graduated cum laude from the M.A in October 2021, and I am very interested in pursuing a Ph.D. in economics in the future.

I have worked on projects with Universidad de los Andes, LSE, Yale University, IDB and Quantil. In doing so, I have been exposed to intensive mathematics and programming challenges related to financial mathematics, forecasting, machine learning, public policy, impact evaluation, among others. Currently, I work as a predoctoral research fellow at Princeton University.
